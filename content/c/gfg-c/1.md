### C Standard
The latest C standard is [ISO/IEC 9899:2011](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C11_(C_standard_revision), also known as [C11](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C11_(C_standard_revision) as the final draft was published in 2011. Before C11, there was C99. The C11 final draft is available [here](https://www.open-std.org/jtc1/sc22/wg14/www/docs/n1570.pdf).
